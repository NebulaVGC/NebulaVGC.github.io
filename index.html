About Me

Welcome. My name is Chase Thompson. I am a sophomore in computer science at Purdue University. I hope you enjoy your stay here!

More About Me

I am a computer science major at Purdue University, pursuing a minor in Japanese and Linguistics. I plan on focusing on machine intelligence as well as software engineering. I hope that I can use my interest in both language/linguistics and machine intelligence to help reduce the error between human and computer communication.

I have a wide array of skills that I wish to expand upon as my career grows. Currently, I have experience with Java, C, C#, Python, and HTML/CSS. I am most comfortable with Java and C, as I have used those languages the most. I also have experience with TensorFlow, as I have had to use machine learning models for some of my projects. I have some experience with the game engine, Unity, as well, as I have attempted to create games with it in C#.

Along with programming languages, I also love learning languages. I am currently learning Japanese at Purdue, but I also have knowledge in Mandarin Chinese and Hindi. I took 4 years of Chinese in high school, and even participated in a Chinese Proficiency Competition and came in 3rd place. I spent some time over the summer to learn Hindi as well, although my knowledge of it is still rather limited. I hope to master these languages in the future to the best of my ability.
